About Me

My name is Sofia Battista and I am an aspiring UX designer. I spent my undergraduate at New York University where I focused in Integrated Digital Media. I have a background in computer science which has fueled my passion to integrate computer coding into my UX designs. Throughout my undergraduate career I have had numerous opportunities to create both hands-on and digital designs which have built up my skills using a wide variety of tools. I am extremely passionate about creating wearable medical devices for individuals with disabilities. Growing up my father worked in construction where he focused on creating more accessible housing and inclusive working environments, in particular focusing on people with disabilities. Over the years I have helped him with these projects and got to see first hand how a disability can affect someone's daily life. My dads years of work inspired me to carry on his efforts in helping those in need by creating devices that help revolutionize medical prosthesis. My goal is to come back to New York City after graduation to work hand in hand with the NYU Langone Center for Amputation Reconstruction to make big strides in the field.
